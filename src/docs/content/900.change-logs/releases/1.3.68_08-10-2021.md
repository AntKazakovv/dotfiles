## 1.3.68 (08-10-2021)

### SCR [#278523](https://tracker.egamings.com/issues/278523) - fix: livechats bugs

1. After the implementation of hiding live chat for unauthorized users, the button in the burger was not hidden
2. when logging out and re-logging in, the default chat button was not hidden from the livechatinc chat, because LivechatButtonComponent did not subscribe to chatstate changes after restarting (when logging in) due to isChatStateWatch, which allowed subscribing only 1 time during the life of LivechatButtonComponent. As a result, LivechatButtonComponent did not subscribe to changes in the state of the new instance of livechatinc
3. In the LivechatButtonComponent in ngOnInit, when subscribing to chatIsHide$, it was not taken into account that the client might not want to hide the default button of laichat
